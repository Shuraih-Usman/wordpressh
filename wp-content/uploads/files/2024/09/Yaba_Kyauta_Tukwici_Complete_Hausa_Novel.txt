_"Allah wadaranki munafuka macuciya,,insha Allahu saikinyi muguwar nadama arayuwarki,banza wacce batasan mutumci ba",,_


_"Indai akan dukiyar Alhaji ne kika min haka sha Allahu bakida kwai aduniya bareki haihu kici gado,,d'a kuwa sai dai  kingani a hannun wasu mararar imani kawai,,wacce ta rako mata duniya,,kinga wadannan yaran,(ta nuna yaranda ke  kwance kan gado suna bacci basusan me ake ba)saikin nemi taimakonsu insha Allahu",,_


_Dariya Samira ta tuntsire da ita tace "oho dai burinmu yacika nida mahaifiyata,,ke kin zata dan Allah umma  take rike dake,,to ki canja tunani,,dan kina 'Yar wan baba bawai babanne ya haifekiba to dan haka adebi yara a koma gefen bola tunda miji  ya kwace komai nasa",, ta kara she fa shekewa da dariya tayi  waje,,_


_Yaran ta takallah 'yan wata 5 wadanda suke  su 3duka maza,,takara fashewa da kuka,,a fili ta daga hannunta sama tace,,(" ya Allah gani gareka kafi kowa sanina ni marainiyace mara gata Allah kaine gatana ka dubeni da wadannan bayi naka ya Allah karkasa su tozarta")_


_Muryar Alhaji taji akanta yana fadin au baku fitaba da wadannan shegun.......makalewa sauran maganarsa tayi sakamakon  wata razananniyar tsawa data buga masa,,tsawadda saida tasa samira fita aguje dama ita ta zugo Alhajin tace masa gatacan ta zauna taki fita,,,,_


_Tana huci tace"zanjure komai a duniyar nan,, bama daga  wurinkaba daga gurin kowama amma banda kiran 'ya'yana shegu dan haka ka iya bakin ka,,tunda ka koreni kabari zan fita ay"_


_Sum sum Alhaji ya fice da wallahi tsawar tashigesa,,samira ma bata kara fitowaba amma tana makale jikin windo tana jiran fitarta,,dan boka yace inda alfijir ya keto bata fitaba to asirin zai karye,,_


_Katon akwati ta dauko ta loda kayan yaranta a ciki,,nata  kadan ta diba ta zuba musu duk wani abin bukatarsu,,kwalayen madararsu ta diba ta zuba ta windo  suka fada kasa yadda inta fita zata dauka dan batason yaranta susha wahalar rayuwa zatayi iya yinta dan inganta rayuwarsu,,_


_Biyu ta diba ta goya a bayanta,,sannan ta goya daya ta gaba,,katon hijabinta ta saka sannan ta dauki jakar hannunta tajawo akwatinta ta fita_


_Masu aikin gidan tagani sunata sharar hawaye,,itama goge nata tayi,,bata kula kowaba ta fice  waje  lokacin gari yayi tsit,,12 saura kadan kuji wata masifa_


_Samira naganin fitarta tayi wata shewa tajanyo wayarta ta dokawa,,(tabawa)ummanta kenan kira,,,dama itama kiran 'yar  tata take jira dan haka dasauri ta daga,,"Hello Umma ta tafi,,, ta tafi,,burina  yacika saurake in tazo kimata korar kare "cewar samira  kenan data ji  ummanta ta daga wayar_


_" kwantar da hankalinki 'yata  indai raye dole nane saina rabaki da duk wani abu da bakya so,,tana zuwa nan zansa amata ihun karuwa tazo da 'ya'yan zina",,"ina yinki ummata bari naje wajen Alhaji",,,"to kitabbatar kinyi komai yanda (boka na gangare) yace",,,"to umma  a ankare nake  ay",,,to shikenan saida  safe_


_Fitowarta daga gidan dai dai da tasowar wani gagarumin hadari,,gabanta ne  ya fadi data tuna in ruwannan ya taba *HAISAM* tofa tashiga ukunta,,dan shi bama kullum take mai wankaba saboda lura datai yanada cutar sanyi,,iskar dake kadawa ce,,takara karfi alamar ruwa na nan tafe,,tunowa data yi da ajiyarta abayan katangar dakinta tasa ta juyawa ta nufi bayan gidan,,akanta ta dora  kwalayen uku taja akwatin ta cigaba da tafiya tan addu'ar Allah yasa tasamu mafaka,,kafin ruwan ya sauka,,_


_Bakin titi ta fita amma shiru ba  kowa harta fita express,, wani mai akorikura ne daya lodo itace yazo zai wuce tayi saurin taresa,,bai kiba ya tsaya tace  ya taimaka ya rage mata hanya,,_


_To yace a gaba  yasata yaronsa ya debi  kayanta ya hau bayan motar,,mai motar bai mata maganaba duk da yaganta tanata sharar hawaye,,_

3⃣&4⃣


_Daga babban titin unguwar asokoro ta Abuja basu tsaya a ko inaba sai kaduna unguwan sunusi,anan mutumin yayi parking ya kalleta yace,"baiwar Allah iyakata nan in zaki kara gabane to kihau abin hawa anan yakaiki tasha",_


_harta bude baki zatace inane nan,sai taga wani syn board an rubuta Kaduna unguwan unguwan sunusi kusada wata mai abinci duk da alokacin darene dan 4bataiba,cikin muryar sanyi tace "nagode nan ma yayi", sannan ta sauko yaron motar ya aje mata kayan ta,mai motar ua rufe motarsa ya gangara kasan anguwar,,_


_itama gafe taja kayanta kan wani dan dandamali ta zauna,alokacin dan dake daure gabanta yafara motsawa yagaji dan damashi yafi y'an uwansa rigima kuma shine babba,,_


_A binda bata saniba yaron motar mutuminnan bai tafiba yana labe yana kallonta dan yaga alamar batasan kowaba,,gidan su  ya nufa ya kwankwasa, babarsa ce ta bude masa tana "sannu dazuwa Dantala",,da yauwa ya amsa mata,,sannan yace "gwaggo zo kiga wata mata tana bukatar taimako yaranta 3 tafito a wannan daran kuma da alamar matsalace ta fito da ita bata da inda zata,kije kizo da ita  nan kodan 'ya'yanta,"_


_Hijab gwaggon Dantala tasa tadauko fitila irin  mai kalanzir dinnan,, suka fito,,gurinta suka nufa sallama gwaggo tamata tace" baiwar Allah lafiya kika zauna anan  ga yara ko kin manta gidan da zaki"?maimakon tayi magana kawai saita fashe da kuka,,ganin haka da gwaggo tai yasata fadin "subahananilillah yarinya daina kuka taso muje" ta dauki d'an da ke kan kafarta data sau koshi,,Dantala ya kwashi kayan kayan suka wuce har cikin gidan,,a tsakaddakin gwaggo Dantala ya aje kayan,,taimaka mata gwaggo tayi ta sauko sauran na bayanta,aka kwantar dasu akujerar dakin irin ta da dinnan,,_


_Dakin dake ciki gwaggo ta shige tadan kara gyara mata gadon karfen,,sannan ta fito ta kwashe yaran takai ciki,ta dawo kallonta tayi tana nan tsaye"taho muje ki kwanta yarinya,,a hankali tabita cikin dakin,,gado ta nuna mata tace"hau ki kwanta kinji da safe maye magana",,_


_Kamar wadda kwai ya fashewa aciki haka tahau ta kwanta kusa da yaranta,,gwaggo kuma ta fita,,kayan tashiga dasu dakin,,sannan ta fito ta fara hada surfen waken kosan siyarwar datake,,ganin asuba ta taho ba sauran bacci a wurinta'',_


_Ita kuwa Aisha bata runtsaba tadai kwantane kawai,,banda bugu mai karfi babu abinda zuciyarta keyi,,tunani ne fal ranta na yadda 'yar uwarta ta kwace mata miji,,kuma ta rabata da gidanma baki daya,abinda yasa ma bataje gidan suba wurin ummansu tasan bakinsu daya  da 'yarta to intaje ma baza taji da dadi ba,shi  yasa ta zabi shiga duniya tasan na Allah basa karewa,,_


_Hawaye takeyi kamar me ranta na zafi,,a haka har akai assalatu,,dai dai da tashin dukkansu yaran suka buga wani uban kuka,,dasauri ta tashi zaune,,gwaggo ma da sauri tashigo tana fadin muntashi ay basai kun tadamuba",_


_Murmushi tayi tace,"ay haka suke dukkansu suke tashi da zarar ankira assalatu"dariya gwaggo tayi ta dauko daya ta fito dama ta hada ruwan wanka tamasa,,sauranma tayi musu,,sannan tace ma mamansu kema jekiyi ga bayin can,,_


_Kayan wankanta datazo dasu ta diba taje tayo,,yaran takallah da da suke jere kan gadon gwaggo tashiryasu tsaf har abinsu tabasu,,gasunan sai wantsala kafa suke,,banda dayan da yay tsit gasa ido bude amma baya ko motsi,,_


_Sai da tai sallah sanna ta zauna tanata addu a da kukanta tana rokon saukin rayuwa ita da y'ay'anta,,_


_Gwaggo kuwa kosanta ta dora a kofar gida,,tuni aka fara cikowar siya abin mamaki yabata ganin yadda make zuba layi,,har markade takara bayarwa ganin wancan zai kare kuma ga jama a_


_Sala makociyarta dake gefe mutanen wurinta kadan ne,,bakin ciki ya ishi sala ganin duk masu siyan kosan wurinta sun koma wurin gwaggo,kuma dama abin mutanene kawai kosan gwaggo yafi na sala dadi da arha ga tsafta,_


_Saida gwaggo tagama tsaf sannan takwashe komai nata takai cikin gida,,shayi ta hadama Aisha da biredi da kosan,,saida ta matsa mata sannan taci,saka ce tashigo tana bala in wai gwaggo tayi surkulle ta kwace mata kasuwa,,_


_Gwaggo ce tafito tace "ke sala ki iya bakinki dama ana kwacewa wani kasuwa,,ba Allah ne kebayarwa" da masifa sala tace "eh nace kin kwace da banafiki yin ciniki ba amma yau banyiba sai uban kwantai danayi",_


_Dariya gwaggo tayi tace " dama ba cinikinki bane Allah ne ya karya alkadarinki dan haka fice min daga gida konasa Dantala ya zaneminke",_


_Sumi sumi sala ta fita dan duk iskancinta tana tson Dantala wallahi tsaf zai zaneta kan gwaggonsa,_

_"Wallahi ALHAJI SABO bazan bada auran y'ata ga dankaba haka kawai yarinyar mai tarbiya kace nabawa danka ya aura bayan kasan duk wasu halayen banza dankane kan gaba akansu",,_

_Wayoyinsa ya kwasa ya fice a fusace,yayinda yabar Alhaji sabo a yay mutuwar tsaye dan baisan abokin nasa yasan wannan sirrin ba,,kuma tabbas yasan wacece UMAIMA shu umar kantace,,amma yaso ya amince tunda yay taurin kai kawai bari yasa a kaddamar masa,,wayar ya dauka yay kira ya kara a kunnensa,,"ku bishi hanyar babban jeji yanzu ya fita daga gidana baiyi nisaba"haka kawai yace sannan ya kashe wayar yana "fadin bakasan waye niba da kayi saurin amsawa abokina,amma taurin kai yaja  maka",,_


_Alhaji haruna indai zai je gidan Alhaji sabo,, baya tafiya da masu gadin lafiyarsa,,hakan yasa shi yake tuki dakansa,,ransa a mutukar yana mamakin Alhaji sabo,,dukkansu masu arziki ne amma shi ya adawa da nasa,,meyasa meyasa san zuciya Allah ya kiyashemu,,_


_Bai yaji yayi karo da abu da kyar motar ta tsaya,,budewa yayi ya dafe kirjinsa dake bugun fitar hayyaci,,bai gama mai da numfashi ba yaga mutane su 5 sun zagayeshi,,suna dariya da mamaki ya kallesu a dan dake yace "lafiyanku kuwa ko kuna bukatar wani abune''?_


_"Eh" cewar wanda kamar shine babban su,,"mekuke bukata",,ka amince y'arka ta auri d'an Alhaji sabo",,dariya yayi yace "bai gaya muku bana magana biyu ba",, afusace yace" bazadai ka amince ba ko kai taurin kai ko",,kunamar bindigarsa ya dana,,ya saita goshin alhaji haruna,,_

_"Sir nagaya maka iya gaskiyata ban san inda *ALINA* takeba tabbas taje gidana jiya kuma daga nan bansan inda taje ba",,a matukar har gitse birgediya ya mike tsaye ",_


 _*CAPTAIN HAISAM*,nabaka yau da gobe ka nemo min yarinyata inaba hakaba zansa a harbe min kai,,tashi kafita anan,,"ya fada yana buga table din dake gabansa",_


_A nutse yay salute nasa ya juya ya fice,,abokin sa captain habib yagani yana jiransa dama tare suka zo,,jerawa sukayi suna tafiya amma babu wanda yace komai,,kasan cewar habib yasan halin HAISAM indai bai masa maganaba to shifa bayi masa zai ba,,_


_Cikin murya mai sanyi habib yace,," captain lafiya kuwa?naga ranka kamar abace,,saida yaga dama sannan yace "kasan 'yar iskar yarinyar nan Alina tun jiya bata koma gida ba shine ubanta ya kirani wai na fito masa da y'arsa kaji sai kace wani karamin abu da za a iya boye shi",,_


_A jiyar zuciya habib ya sauke yace" gaskiya yarinyar nan batai dacen ubaba,,taya za ayi kizama karuwa agaban mahaifinki kuma ya rabu dake,,Allah wadaran irin  wadannan iyayen",,_

_Bayan mintuna suka fito sanye da kayan hutu na sojoji,,suka fice,duk inda yakamata su duba ko zasuga alina sun duba basu gantaba,,duk wasu kawayenta da abokan shashancinta sun bin cikesu babu wanda ma yaganta adaren jiya,,_


_Da mamaki habib da HAISAM suka dubi juna,,gida suka koma sukai shirin bacci sannan ya dauko wayar ya kira gida_


_kwana biyu yana nemanta bai sametaba,da daddare ya nufi gidan birgediya dan sanar masa bai gantaba ya bincika wani wurin inda yasan tana zuwa ba ciki barek din ba,,_


_Harya shiga gidan baiji motsin kowaba,,a falo ya zauna yana jiransa yaji shirun yayi yawa ya mike zai fita sai yaji kamar magana a dakin waje na gidan,,a hankali ya karasa bakin windon dakin,,ya leka_


_birgediya ya gani da wani mutum mai bakaken kaya ga alina kwance kamar ma ta mutu,,muryar birgediya yaji yana cewa"ka cire mata duk abnida kuke bukata saimu ce yaronne ya mata haka",,yana gama fadar hakan sai ya fito,,_

_Ciki sanyin jiki habib yace"hakane dan uwana amma saina rakaka bakin wannan jejin",,,"ah ah bros kayi zaman ka yanzu ido za saka maka da zarar kayi wani motsin fita za a ce bakinmu daya dan haka maida jakarka",,_ 


_komawa yayi dakin yadawo suka fito tare,,har bakin get,,rungume juna sukai habib kamar yay kuka yakeji,,dakyar sukai sallama,,habib ya koma shi kuma yay gaba dan danisa daga jejin zuwa bakin titi,,_

 _Cikin seconds ya danata shima ya saitashi,,nanfa aka fara kallon kallo tsakanisa da mutanen,,dake bai son jira bane tuni ya harbi daya a kafa sun yun kuro yakara masa,,ay dasauri sukai baya sunga hauka sunsan bazasu iya da wannan ba dan da ganinsa sojane,,ga alamu nan ajikinsa duk da ba kayan yasa ba amma takalmin kafarsa da jakar bayansa zasu baka shaida,,_


_Basuyi maganaba suka jan ye ohansa dayasha harbi biyu babu gaira babu dalili wannan wane irin mutum ne mai makon ya tsaya asa santa kawaisai harbi,,_


_Suna gama guduwa ya juya ga mutumin ya gansa ya zuba masa ido,,murmushi yayi masa yace"sannu daddy basu maka komaiba ko?"_


_Murmushi Alhaji yayi yace"basu min komaiba dana,,nagode da taimakon ka",,"karka damu muje na raka ka gida",baiyi musuba captain ya shiga mazaunin driver Alhaji ya shiga daya barayin,,"ina zamuyi"cewar captain "Asokoro" kawai Alhaji yace,,_
